Frequently asked questions

How do I know if a restaurant accepts Foodie Card?

All participating restaurants should have a green sticker on their window that states they are a Foodie Card member. If you are unsure, please click here.

Can I give my Foodie Card to another person if I'm not attending?

Absolutely not. The Foodie Card holder must be present. Restaurants will make sure the Foodie Card member is at the table whether it's by matching a drivers license or a credit card.

How much is a Foodie Card?

It's $29.99 a year.

How much is donated to Island Harvest?

For every card purchased, Foodie Card donates 5% of the sale to Island Harvest. That is enough to give 1 person an entire day of meals (including breakfast, lunch and dinner).

I lost my card, how do I get a replacement?

Please click here to request a replacement card.

How do I add a restaurant to Foodie Card?

Please click here to submit a restaurant.

How is Foodie Card affiliated with the Tri-State Restaurant Club?

Neil Foster started the Tri-State Restaurant Club in 2015 and also co-founded Foodie Card.

Once a Foodie Card is purchased, when will I receive it in the mail?

It usually takes 7-10 business days.

What should I do if I ate at a restaurant that is supposed to take Foodie Card, but they refused to accept it?

Please click here to file a complaint.

Does Foodie Card cover take-out and delivery?

No, as of now, Foodie Card only works when are you eating at the restaurant.

Are there black-out dates?

A restaurant has the right to refuse a Foodie Card on the following holidays: Christmas Eve,Easter, Father's Day, Mother's Day, New Year's Eve, Thanksgiving Eve, Valentine's Day

Can multiple cards be used on a single bill?

Only one card can be applied per bill.

Is there a maximum discount on a single use?

Yes, the maximum that can be saved in $100.

Can the card be used in combined with other coupons?

No, the Foodie Card cannot be combined with other coupons.

My Foodie Card is about to expire. What should I do?

Nothing – just sit back and relax. You're all set. We'll send you an email a week before and again at least 24 hours before notifying you that the credit card on file will be charged a renewal fee of $29.99 (plus tax).

How does renewals work? Will I be automatically renewed?

Very similar to the expiration question above. Foodie Card prides itself on transparency which is why we notify the customer a week before and again 24 hours before the actual renewal charge of $29.99 (plus tax). Please know that we do not automatically renew – we email twice before a renewal should occur, and all that needs to happen to cancel a renewal is an email to hello@foodiecard.com. As always, we donate a full day of meals to those less fortunate through our partnership with Island Harvest.
