Product Details Foodie Card costs $29.99 a year and provides members with 10% off their bill at tons of incredible restaurants. Also, Foodie Card members have access to special member-only events.

What is the 'Buy A Card, Give A Meal' program? For every Foodie Card purchased, we donate an entire day of meals to somebody in need. To date, we’ve donated close to 20,000 meals.

Any Restrictions? Foodie Card can only be applied to dine-in bills, drinks included. If a restaurant allows Foodie Card to be applied to take-out or delivery, that’s a special benefit that is unrelated to the guidelines of Foodie Card. Participating restaurants reserve the right to refuse Foodie Card on the following holidays: Christmas Eve, Easter, Father’s Day, Mother’s Day, New Year’s Eve, Thanksgiving Eve, and Valentines Day. There is a maximum discount of $100 per bill. Foodie Card cannot be combined with other coupons or specials.
